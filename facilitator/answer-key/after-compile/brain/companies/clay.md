New-York-based GTM data and automation platform, and the anchor sponsor target for GTM Tech Week London.
Clay opened its first London office in March 2026 and is treating Europe as its priority for the next 18
months. The deal is warm but format-gated: Clay won't do a generic booth, but is into co-creating a
GTM-engineering track plus a Clay Club London tie-in. As of 2026-06-20 the co-created track is sold
internally and it's "basically a yes in principle" once the operator-review commitment is in writing.
