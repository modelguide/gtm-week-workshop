## Transcript

**Artur Wala:** Bruno, thanks for making time. We're taking GTM Tech Week to London and you were top of
our list — partly because, well, you opened the office in March.

**Bruno Estrella:** Ha, yeah. London's the priority for the next eighteen months. Europe's already
about a fifth of revenue and until March we basically had no one here. So the timing's right for us.

**Krzysztof Pawlak:** That's exactly why we reached out. The honest pitch is — you kind of invented the
category we named the event after. GTM engineering. It'd be strange for you *not* to be on that stage.

**Bruno Estrella:** I like that framing. I'll be straight with you though — the generic sponsor thing,
booth and a logo, that's not really how we do events. We run Sculpt, we run the Clay Clubs. When we
show up we want to actually teach something.

**Artur Wala:** Totally. So what if it's not a booth. What if it's a curated GTM-engineering track —
you help us pick the talks, they get reviewed by operators, no pitch decks on stage. And we tie it to a
Clay Club London the night before.

**Bruno Estrella:** Now you're speaking my language. That I can sell internally. The budget's honestly
not the hard part — we have a partner fund that can cover event spend. The hard part is always format.
If it reads as sponsored content the GTM crowd switches off.

**Krzysztof Pawlak:** Agreed, and we'd kill the brand if we let that happen. "Not more AI SDR spam" is
literally on our homepage.

**Bruno Estrella:** Good. One thing — watch the dates. Sculpt's October eighth. We won't want London
sitting right on top of it. Spring's probably cleaner for us.

**Artur Wala:** Noted, spring's where we're leaning anyway. I'll send you a one-pager — co-created
track, the Club tie-in, and a couple of date options. Who else should see it?

**Bruno Estrella:** Send it to me first. We've got a founding EMEA marketer starting in July — once
they're in, they'll own this, and London community is literally in their job title. I'll hand it over
warm.

**Krzysztof Pawlak:** Perfect. We'll get that over this week.

**Bruno Estrella:** Sounds good. Genuinely into this — keep it operator-first and we're in the
conversation.
