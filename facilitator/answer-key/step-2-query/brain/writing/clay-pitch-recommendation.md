# Clay — pitch recommendation

**Question:** should we pitch Clay a standard sponsorship package, and who is the first contact?

**Short answer: no standard package. Pitch co-creation, and start with Bruno Estrella.**

## Why not a standard package
- Clay runs its own events (Sculpt, 60+ Clay Clubs) and curates them; it owns events more than it buys
  booths [Source: account-deep-dive_ANCHOR]. On the intro call Bruno was lukewarm on a generic
  sponsorship and explicit that "the generic sponsor thing, booth and a logo, that's not really how we
  do events" [Source: 2026-06-18-clay-sponsorship-intro].
- The blocker is format, not budget: Clay's Partner Growth Fund can cover event spend, but talks must be
  operator-reviewed with no sponsored content on stage [Source: 2026-06-18-clay-sponsorship-intro].

## What to pitch instead
- A co-created GTM-engineering track plus a Clay Club London tie-in the night before
  [Source: 2026-06-18-clay-sponsorship-intro]. This is on-message: Clay created and funds the "GTM
  engineer" category the event is named after [Source: account-deep-dive_ANCHOR].
- The timing is the pitch: Clay opened its first London office in March 2026, Europe is ~20% of revenue,
  and a founding EMEA team is hiring now — a London GTM event is on their critical path
  [Source: account-deep-dive_ANCHOR].

## First contact
- **Bruno Estrella**, Head of Growth Marketing — he took the intro and runs Clay's content engine
  [Source: account-deep-dive_ANCHOR]. He will hand the relationship to the incoming EMEA Founding
  Marketer (~July), who will own London community [Source: 2026-06-18-clay-sponsorship-intro].
