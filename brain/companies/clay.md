Clay (clay.com) is a top sponsor target for GTM Tech Week London 2027 — they effectively defined the "GTM engineering" category the event is named after. They opened a London office in March 2026 and have named Europe their priority for the next ~18 months, so the timing is aligned. They are warm but will **not** buy a generic booth/logo package: their model is owned, curated, teach-something events (Sculpt, Clay Clubs). The live opportunity is a **co-created GTM-engineering track + a Clay Club London tie-in**, operator-reviewed with no sponsored content on stage. Budget is not the blocker (a Partner Growth Fund can cover event spend) — **format** is. As of the 20 June follow-up this is close to a **yes in principle**: Bruno sold the track internally, the one remaining ask is that the one-pager put in writing that **operators (not Clay) review the talks**.
