What gtm-week.com sells, at what price. Two revenue lines: **sponsorship** (to companies in the [[icp-gtm-week]]) and **tickets** (to the audience that makes the room worth buying). The through-line is the same brand rule that qualifies the ICP — *not more AI SDR spam*, operator-reviewed, no pitch decks on stage. All prices are **fixed in GBP** ("no starting at", "early bird is a date, not a gimmick"); the best sponsors **co-create** rather than buy a logo wall [Source: content-library/sponsorship-prospectus.md] [Source: content-library/ticket-launch-landing-block.md].

## State

### Sponsorship packages (the revenue offer to companies)
All figures fixed GBP; co-marketing add-ons priced per case [Source: content-library/sponsorship-prospectus.md]:

| Tier | £ (GBP) | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **Headline Partner** | £45,000 | One only. Co-create a track. Top billing across the week. Keynote-adjacent. |
| **Platinum** | £28,000 | A session or workshop slot, premium presence, curated intros. |
| **Gold** | £16,000 | A roundtable or workshop, strong presence, intros. |
| **Silver** | £9,000 | Presence plus a dinner seat where the real conversation happens. |
| **Side-event Partner** | £7,500 | Co-host an official side event (Club, breakfast, dinner). |
| **Startup / Community** | £4,500 | For early teams. A seat in the room without the enterprise price. |

The pitch: "the room you have been trying to buy your way into with ads, in one place, for one week." Not next to the content — where it fits, *in* it. The best sponsors don't buy a booth, they co-create (a curated track, a workshop their team runs, a side event with their name on it) [Source: content-library/sponsorship-prospectus.md].

### Tickets (the audience offer)
Prices real and fixed; early bird is a date, not a gimmick. Maps to `luma_ticket-sales.csv` ticket types [Source: content-library/ticket-launch-landing-block.md]:

| Pass | £ (GBP) | For |
|---|---|---|
| **Early Bird** | £295 | Limited. Same pass as Standard, before the price goes up. |
| **Standard** | £495 | Full week. All rooms. |
| **Operator Pass** | £395 | In-seat GTM, sales, RevOps and marketing operators. |
| **Founder Pass** | £195 | Early-stage founders running their own GTM. |
| **Team Pass (4)** | £1,580 | The team that owns the motion, together. |

The room is small on purpose — quality of room over size of room [Source: content-library/ticket-launch-landing-block.md].

### The ask (how the offer is presented)
"Reply with the tier you are weighing and one line on what you want out of the room. We will send a short plan, not a 40-slide deck." [Source: content-library/sponsorship-prospectus.md]
