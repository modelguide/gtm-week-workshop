# Gaps — open questions, contradictions, things to chase

> **Workshop TL;DR (for humans):** Whenever the brain notices something unresolved — two sources that
> disagree, a missing fact, a "we should check this" — it writes one line here instead of guessing. When
> you run a health check (lint), the problems it finds land here.
