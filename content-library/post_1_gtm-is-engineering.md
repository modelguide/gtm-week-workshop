# Post: "GTM is becoming an engineering discipline"

Most GTM teams added AI the same way they added every tool before it. On top.

A new SDR tool here. An AI writer there. A scraper, a sequencer, an enrichment tab nobody owns. More
activity. More outbound. More dashboards. Still no system.

That is the gap. The companies pulling ahead are not the ones with the most tools. They are the ones
who wired sales, marketing, ops and data into one motion, with the AI inside the system instead of
bolted to the side of it.

That is what we mean by GTM engineering. Not a rebrand of RevOps. A discipline. Company context,
workflows, signals, evals and human review, built and maintained like infrastructure.

GTM Tech Week is a week for the people doing that work. Founders, GTM leaders, operators turning
scattered AI experiments into a revenue system.

Not a keynote about the future. The rooms where the build happens.
